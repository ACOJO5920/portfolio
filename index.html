Photography

It being 2021, it's incredible to see how far smartphone cameras have come since the inception of the iPhone. It's mad to think that the original iPhone didn't even have autofocus, you just hoped that it would catch the subject!

There's a saying in the world of smartphone photography: "The best camera in the world is the one you carry with you" It's funny to think about that, because i actually have a full-size reasonably expensive camera at home, some model of Canon EOS-whatever. But do I take it anywhere? Hell no. I've not got time for that.

All the photos you'll see on this page were taken by a smartphone camera, either my old Honor 9, Oneplus 6, or my current iPhone Xr
